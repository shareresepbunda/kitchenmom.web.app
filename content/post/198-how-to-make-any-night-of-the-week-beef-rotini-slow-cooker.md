1. Make ready 2 tsp garlic, minced
1. Take 1/2 tsp salt

1. Get 3 cup water
1. Make ready 1 Tbsp extra virgin olive oil
1. Prepare 2 cup rotini noodles, uncooked
1. Get 1/2 cup heavy whipping cream
1. Take 1 1/2 cups Mozzarella cheese, shredded
