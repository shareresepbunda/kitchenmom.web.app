##### The ingredients needed to make Key Lime Custard Pie with Oreo Crust:

##### Steps to make Key Lime Custard Pie with Oreo Crust:

1. Squeeze some lime juice .
1. Beat egg yolks.

1. Mix all together.
1. Pour the mixture into the crust and bake for 20-25 mins
